🔍 How to Test AI Search

1. Click the floating search trigger in the bottom-right corner or press Ctrl + K (or Cmd + K).
2. Type a natural language query, such as "something gold for a wedding" or "noise canceling ears".
3. Check out the matched suggestions dropdown and click on a search result card.
4. Note: To see items, first upload your product feed (e.g. CSV) via the **Merchant Console** and copy your **Store ID** into this sandbox page URL query string (e.g. ?store_id=your-store-id).
